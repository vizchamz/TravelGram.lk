Amazing Galle

HISTORICAL & HERITAGE TOURS

Situated on the southwest coast of Sri Lanka, the city of Galle is the go-to destination
for many travellers on this marvellous island. With its beautiful collection of Dutch colonial buildings
and rocky coastline, Galle exudes charm and sophistication as well as a unique cultural identity,
shaped by centuries of different influences. The main fort area is the centrepiece of this
city and has a number of quaint boutiques and cafes lining the beautiful, palm-fringed streets.
